About The Movement

A Global Movement Inspired By Deborah

The Deborah Network exists to raise courageous women who lead with wisdom, influence with integrity, and transform generations through faith-driven leadership.

Inspired by the biblical Deborah—a prophetess, judge, strategist, and nation-builder—we exist to awaken purpose, cultivate leadership, and create lasting impact in families, communities, ministries, businesses, and governments.

  • ✓ A house of refuge for the battered, injured & vulnerable
  • ✓ An umbrella advocating women's spiritual, emotional & social well-being
  • ✓ A community where women express their God-given talents & callings
